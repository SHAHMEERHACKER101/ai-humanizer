What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, which can make your next visit easier and the site more useful to you.

How We Use Local Storage

NexusRank Pro primarily uses browser local storage instead of traditional cookies. Local storage allows us to store information directly in your browser for the following purposes:

Usage Tracking

  • • Track your free tool usage (1 use per tool limit)
  • • Remember which tools you've already used
  • • Reset usage counters when appropriate

Pro User Authentication

  • • Store your Pro user login status
  • • Remember your authenticated session
  • • Provide seamless access to unlimited tools

User Preferences

  • • Remember your preferred settings
  • • Store interface customizations
  • • Maintain your user experience preferences

Third-Party Services

Our website may use the following third-party services that may set their own cookies:

Google Fonts

We use Google Fonts to enhance the visual appearance of our website. Google may set cookies to optimize font loading and performance.

Font Awesome

We use Font Awesome for icons throughout our interface. This service may set cookies for performance optimization.

Cloudflare

Our website is protected and accelerated by Cloudflare, which may set cookies for security and performance purposes.

Managing Your Data

Local Storage Control

You have full control over the local storage data we use. You can:

  • • Clear your browser's local storage at any time
  • • Use browser incognito/private mode to prevent storage
  • • Disable local storage in your browser settings
  • • Contact us to reset your usage data

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies and local storage through their settings. You can typically:

  • • Block all cookies and local storage
  • • Delete existing cookies and stored data
  • • Set notifications when cookies are being set
  • • Choose which sites can store data

Data Retention

Local storage data persists until you clear it manually or through browser settings. We do not automatically expire this data, as it's essential for tracking your free usage limits and maintaining your Pro user status.

Privacy and Security

All data stored locally on your device remains under your control and is not transmitted to our servers except when necessary to provide our services. We use industry-standard security practices to protect any data that is transmitted.
